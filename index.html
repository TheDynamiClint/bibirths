Services

Doula Care

A certified, experienced doula with years of supporting women and families will provide exceptional service to you through regular physical, mental and emotional support during labor and birth, on-call availability, unlimited phone and email contact, two prenatal visits and one postpartum to your location of choice.

Placenta Encapsulation

Placentas are prepared by cleansing with Ph balanced and sterilized water, then sliced, dehydrated, pulverized into fine powder, and encapsulated. Careful methodology provides mothers with capsules comprised of the most select tissue. Other constituents can be compounded into salves or tinctures for an additional fee or kept to bury as a gift back to the earth.
